BNC Server Rules

  • Respect Everyone
    Be kind and polite. No bullying, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks.
  • No Spam or Self-Promotion
    Avoid repeated messages, unnecessary links, or ads unless permitted.
  • Stay On Topic
    Use the correct channels and keep discussions relevant.
  • No Harmful or Inappropriate Content
    No violent, illegal, explicit, or disturbing content.
  • Protect Privacy
    Do not share personal or sensitive information.
  • Use Appropriate Language
    Avoid excessive swearing or offensive language.
  • Follow Moderator Instructions
    Respect staff decisions and cooperate.
  • No Trolling or Drama
    Do not provoke conflict or spread negativity.
  • Report Problems, Don’t Escalate
    Report rule-breaking instead of arguing.
  • Have Fun & Contribute Positively
    Help build a positive community.
  • No Politics
    Political topics are not allowed to avoid conflict.
  • AI Content Restriction
    AI-related content is prohibited unless for jokes or memes.
  • TCC Strictly Prohibited
    Any TCC-related content is not allowed under any circumstances.
⚠️ Consequences: Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or permanent bans depending on severity.
